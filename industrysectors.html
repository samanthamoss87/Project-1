Industry Sectors

RS Vision Consulting specialise in the industry sectors below from Board Level Management, Marketing, Sales, Mid-level Leadership and Recruitment to Technology, Hardware, Software Development and Strategy.

Automotive

With more than 20+ years of Automotive sector experience across dealerships, Auction Houses, Leasing companies and OEM's, our team can provide expert guidance to our Automotive Partners on video/telematics, hardware, software, installation services and legislation including DVS 2024.

Benefiting from extensive telematics industry experience, we can help your business to source, on-board and integrate the best video telematics technology. With rapidly growing customer demands for this technology, video telematics products can also generate additional, and recurring revenue streams.

In addition, we also provide guidanceto telematics businesses seeking to better understand this sector and build products and services to meet the customer and supplier demands.

Video Telematics

We are subject matter experts on video telematics, before starting RS Vision Consulting, our leadership team were instrumental in the creation and expediential growth of the Europe’s market leading video telematics providers.

Our team have extensive experience within Sales, Marketing, Product, Software / Platform Development, Commercial, Management, Installations & Operations.

OEM

With more than 20 years of experience in working with Plant, Equipment and Vehicle manufacturers / OEM’s, in respect of the provision of Video Telematics, camera and vehicle safety solutions. From production line fit through to aftermarket parts sales, we can provide extensive and wide-ranging expertise in this area to ensure your business is able to on-board and support the latest in-vehicle safety technology.


The solutions we can deliver will meet your customer and legislative demands including DVS 2024.


Our services cover product selection, product test management, warranty friendly vehicle integration (Plug & Play installation Looms), Product certifications for compliance & Type Approval, Training, Sales & Marketing, Pricing, Strategy and more. We can also support video enablement / integration of video into your existing OEM telematics platform.

Telematics

Our management team have more than thirty years of Telematics industry experience, having held senior European leadership roles, within some of the world’s largest telematics providers.


We can provide expertise on many areas from how to video enable your telematics platform, to how to scale and expand your business into new vertical markets or international territories and much more besides.

We work closely with a number of highly skilled and experience outsourced development teams, where we are able to project manage and deliver in areas such as App development, video integration, third party API integrations on your behalf with your customers and partners.

We provide support in areas inlcuding training, sales, performance management and recruitment.
